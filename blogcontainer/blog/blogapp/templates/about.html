About Us

Our Mission

At GiveWell, our mission is simple yet profound: to ensure that no child goes to bed hungry. We believe that every child deserves access to nutritious food, and we are committed to making that belief a reality. Our work centers on alleviating child hunger and malnutrition, one meal at a time.

Health Care

Our organization is dedicated to making a meaningful impact on the lives of those facing health emergencies in hospitals. We are a passionate group of individuals who believe in the power of collective compassion to alleviate suffering and save lives. Our mission is simple yet profound: to provide essential financial support to individuals and families in dire need during times of health crises.

Environment Care

We envision a world where the natural beauty and resources of our planet are cherished, protected, and sustained for generations to come. Our goal is to inspire individuals, communities, and corporations to take responsible actions to safeguard our environment and create a more sustainable future.

Natural Disasters

At Charity Donation for Natural Disasters, our mission is to provide immediate relief and long-term support to those affected by natural disasters around the world. We believe that in times of crisis, compassion and solidarity can make a significant difference in the lives of survivors.
